What is the Environment?

The environment is everything around us.
Examples:
  • Human beings
  • Animals
  • Water
In the environment there are living and non-living things.

The carrying capacity

What is carrying capacity?
The carrying capacity of an environment means how many things can live in an environment without damaging it.

Factors that affect carrying capacity

  • Space
  • Water
  • Food
